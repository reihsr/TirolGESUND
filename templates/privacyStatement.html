Collection of your information

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

Personal Data

Personally identifiable information, such as your name, shipping address, email address, and telephone number, and demographic information, such as your age, gender, hometown, and interests, that you voluntarily give to us when you choose to participate in various activities related to the Site, such as study registration. You are under no obligation to provide us with personal information of any kind, however your refusal to do so may prevent you from using certain features of the Site.

Derivative Data

Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Site, such as your IP address, your browser type, your operating system, your access times, and the pages you have viewed directly before and after accessing the Site.

Mobile Device Data

Device information, such as your mobile device ID, model, and manufacturer, and information about the location of your device, if you access the Site from a mobile device.

Study Device Data

When you register with the TirolGESUND Garmin app - as part of the TirolGESUND study -, activity- and health-related data will be collected in a coded and pseudonymised manner without direct personal information.

Connect Garmin with TirolGESUND

By connecting with your app, you agree to share information from your Garmin Connect account with TirolGESUND. This will enable us to access and analyse data relating to your activity, step count, heart rate, and other activity- or health-related data. Your data will only be stored and analysed in a coded manner (pseudonymised), i.e. not linked to any of your personal data.

After completion of the study you can keep your Garmin Device and remove the connection in the Garmin Connect Settings. If you have any further questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to the study team under info@eutops.at.

Use of your information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site or Garmin Connect app:

  • Compile anonymous statistical data and analysis for use internally or with third parties.
  • Email you regarding your study participation.
  • Increase the efficiency and operation of the Site.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the Site.
  • Garmin TirolGESUND app: health- and activity-related data - only relating to participants of the TirolGESUND study - analysis.
